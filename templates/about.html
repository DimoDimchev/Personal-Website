A small introduction

My name is Dimo Dimchev and I am from Bulgaria. I'm currently in the 11th grade in high-school and will be graduating next year. Apart from school I also attend the Software University of Sofia where I study Software and Web development.

My interest in computer science and programming was born in the 8th grade when I studied Informatics for the first time in my life. As soon as I started studying that subject I knew that I would pursue a career in the IT field. I was fascinated(and still am) by all the possibilities that one has behind the computer screen, and so mu journey began.

At the beginning I had no idea what I was doing. Just like any other beginner I wanted to start making projects right away and skipped a major part of the necessary fundamentals(which I would later regret). For a couple of years I fiddled around with Front-End development but I felt that I no longer was interested in programming so I stopped doing it for a year.

In late 2020 I fell in love with programming and computer science once again and this time I decided to do things the right way. I enrolled in the Software University of Sofia and paid attention to every piece of information that was fed to me. For just under 6 months I managed to go through more than 400 coding problems and watched all the learning materials from the university program(this usually takes more than 2 years) and even managed to complete a short interview at a local company.

Currently I am trying to find a summer internship or even my first time job as a Software/Web developer and am looking forward to all the challenges I will face in this difficult field.
